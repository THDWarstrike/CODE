The Brotherhood has existed since 256 BC. As for tyranny the Assassin's despise it. The Assassin's motto is that nothing is true everything is permitted. Description of every game

Assassin's Creed

In the first game you play as Altaïr Ibn-LaʼAhad and after one failed mission you have to regain your old title. After you gain Master Assassin you become the mentor of the assassin's in Isreal. You will also be in the year of 1191

Assassin's Creed 2

You play as Ezio Auditore da Firenze, during the Italian Renaissance. You are starting your life as assassin. You want revenge for the death of your Father and brothers. You hunt down the men who killed your family.

Assassin's Creed Brotherhood

You play again as Ezio Auditore da Firenze but this time in 16th century rome. This time you are revivng the Brotherhood. You also have to assassinate your enemy family the Barons

Assassin's Creed Revelations

You play one last time as the Assassin mentor Ezio Auditore da Firenze. You play in the year 1511 and you sought out the secret library of Altaïr Ibn-LaʼAhad.

Assassin's Creed III

This game tells the story of Haytham Kenway and Conner Kenway. The era of this game is during the American Revolutionary War. The assassin Conner Kenway is of Native American Heratige.

Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag

This story is set in the 18th century golden age of piracy. It tells the story of Edward Kenway Father of Haytham and grandfather of Conner Kenway. You play in the Caribbean Sea.Also your a pirate.

Assassin's Creed Rouge

This takes place during the seven years war. This is the last game of the kenway saga. It follows the story of assassin-turned-templar Shay Cormac.

Assassin's Creed Unity

Assassin's Creed Unity is a historical Action-Adventure game made in 2013 by Ubisoft. In this game you play as the Assassin Arno Dorian. The setting of the game is 18th century France during the French Revolutionary War. The game in historical sense takes place 6 years after Assassin's Creed III and before Syndicate

Assassin's Creed Syndicate

Assassin's Creed Syndicate is the ninth main installment of the AC franchise. The game takes place 13 years after the passing of Arno Dorian from Unity and it is during the Industrial Revolutionary. You play as the twins Jacob and Evie Frye. Evie rellies on her intellegence and wit while Jacob is that run into the fight type.

Assasson's Creed Orgins

The tine of this game is set in Ancient Egypt. You play as a Madjay(another term for desert-ranger police) Bayek and his wife Aya. You try and protect the people of the Ptolemaic Kingdom during a time of widespread upheaval: the Pharaoh, Ptolemy XIII, struggles to maintain his rule. Bayek's role as a Medjay brings him and Aya into contact with the secretive forces manipulating these events and into becoming the first Assassins.

Assassin's Creed Odyssey

The time set of Odyssey is set in the time of Ancient Greece. It tells the secret history of the Peloponnesian War. You play as the mercanary named Alexios(Will refer to him as Alex for the remander of this.) or Kassandra. You decide who you fight with, Athena or Sparta.

Assassin's Creed Valhalla

Valhalla the most recent game in the franchise. It takes place in historical sense after the events of orgins but before the time of Altiar. You play as the Viking Eivor Varinsdottir. The game has new elements such as forging political alliances with NPC clans. Every choice and decision of the players affects the world of the game in real time. Players can travel from the cold shores of Norway to England. You can use an assortment of weapons and duel-wield a majority of them. You can use the hidden blade in combat as well which hasn't been seen since Orgins. You can build up your settlement ranging from a tattoo shop to the barracks. You can recruit and train for raids and missions.
